# Privacy Policy

Manage your privacy policy content.

## Overview

Create and maintain the privacy policy displayed to users of your loyalty program.

## Accessing Privacy Policy

Navigate to **News & Updates > Privacy Policy**

## Creating Privacy Policy

### Step 1: Click Create

Navigate to **Privacy Policy > Create Privacy Policy Card**

### Step 2: Enter Content

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Section title |
| **Content** | Policy content |
| **Order** | Display sequence |
| **Status** | Active/Inactive |

### Step 3: Save

Click **Save** to publish.

## Editing/Deleting

- **Edit:** Action > Edit to modify
- **Delete:** Action > Delete to remove

## Privacy Policy Sections

Common sections include:
- Information Collection
- Use of Information
- Data Sharing
- Data Security
- User Rights
- Contact Information

## Best Practices
