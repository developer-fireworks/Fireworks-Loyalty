# News & Updates

Manage content, FAQs, news, promotions, and legal pages in the Loyalty CRM system.

## Overview

The News & Updates module provides tools for managing all content displayed to members including FAQs, informational pages, news, promotions, and legal documents.

## Module Components

| Component | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **FAQ** | Frequently asked questions |
| **Informational Pages** | Static content pages |
| **News & Promotions** | News articles and promotions |
| **Banner Management** | Homepage banners |
| **Privacy Policy** | Legal privacy content |
| **Terms & Conditions** | Legal terms content |

Navigate to **News & Updates** from the main menu.

## Content Types

### FAQ
Questions and answers about your loyalty program, organized by category.

### Informational Pages
Static pages for content like:
- About Us
- How It Works
- Contact Information
- Store Directory

### News & Promotions
Dynamic content including:
- Latest news
- Current promotions
- Member privileges
- Event announcements

### Legal Pages
Required legal documentation:
- Privacy Policy
- Terms and Conditions

## Best Practices

{% hint style="tip" %}
**Content Management:**
- Keep content current and relevant

- Include visuals where helpful
- Review content regularly

{% hint style="info" %}

- Use descriptive titles
- Write clear meta descriptions
- Organize content logically
{% endhint %}
