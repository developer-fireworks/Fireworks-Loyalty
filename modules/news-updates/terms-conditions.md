# Terms and Conditions

Manage your terms and conditions content.

## Overview

Create and maintain the terms and conditions for your loyalty program.

## Accessing Terms and Conditions

Navigate to **News & Updates > Terms and Conditions**

## Creating Terms and Conditions

### Step 1: Click Create

Navigate to **Terms and Conditions > Create Terms and Conditions**

### Step 2: Enter Content

| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Title** | Section title |
| **Content** | Terms content |
| **Order** | Display sequence |
| **Status** | Active/Inactive |

### Step 3: Save

Click **Save** to publish.

## Editing/Deleting

- **Edit:** Action > Edit to modify
- **Delete:** Action > Delete to remove

## Common Sections

- Program Eligibility
- Earning Points
- Redeeming Points
- Point Expiration
- Member Responsibilities
- Program Changes
- Termination
- Liability Limitations
