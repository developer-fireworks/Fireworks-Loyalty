# Banner Management

Configure homepage banners and featured content.

## Overview

Banner Management allows you to control what content appears prominently on your app or website homepage.

## Setting Content as Banner

### Step 1: Navigate to Content

Go to **News & Updates > News and Promotions**

### Step 2: Select Item

Find the content you want to feature.

### Step 3: Set as Banner

Click **Set as Banner** on the item.

### Step 4: Configure Banner

| Setting | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Position** | Banner slot number |
| **Start Date** | When to display |
| **End Date** | When to remove |
| **Link** | Click destination |

### Step 5: Save

Click **Save** to activate the banner.

## Removing a Banner

1. Navigate to the banner item
2. Click **Remove as Banner**
3. Confirm removal
